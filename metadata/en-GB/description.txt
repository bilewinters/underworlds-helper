Finally, an app to aid you in playing games of Underworlds!

Track the game state for you and your opponent:

- Never forget the round you are on again!

- Keep track of you and your opponents activations!

- Your glory points at your finger tips - and an eye on how much your opponent has scored!

We hope to introduce more features as we move forwards, and to reintroduce augmented reality for scatter moves.
